How It Works

How Our Medical Billing Process Works

Our streamlined billing cycle helps practices get paid faster with fewer denials — from first contact to final payment.

1
Practice Assessment

We evaluate your workflows, specialty needs, and goals to create a personalized billing strategy.

2
Patient Intake & Verification

We gather patient details and verify insurance eligibility and coverage before services are rendered.

3
Accurate Medical Coding

Certified coders assign precise CPT, ICD-10, and HCPCS codes to ensure clean, accurate claims.

4
Fast Claim Submission

Claims are electronically submitted and scrubbed to minimize delays, rejections, and denials.

5
Payment Posting

We post payments from payers and patients, reconciling balances and ensuring accurate tracking.

6
Reports & Follow-Up

Receive timely payouts and detailed performance reports with full transparency on your revenue cycle.
